The letters spell the answer SOX FANS.

This puzzle was the last to be written for the hunt. It's a good puzzle I think, so I wish it had appeared a little bit earlier. It was ultimately solved by a few teams so that made me happy. On the whole I think this puzzle examplifies the difference between the second and third round. The second round is shorter puzzles with clever extractions, the third round is longer puzzles with simpler extractions. There's a poll about this on the feedback form! We might tilt next year's hunt in one of these directions. -JO
