Each tale describes an episode of SpongeBob SquarePants from the perspective of a character in the episode. The episodes are clued in alphabetical order. Each tale also has a clue at the end of it that clues the name of another episode in the list.

Each tale also contains a number in it--a number that is incorrect and exaggerated. Figure out how much each narrator is stretching the truth by and index into their name.

The episodes can be ordered into a closed loop by connecting the tailing epsiode to a described epsidode. Starting with Help Wanted, the first episode ever, the extracted letters spell NEMOS SUB. Captain Nemo's sub, the NAUTLIUS, is the answer to the puzzle.

Author's Notes
I grew up a big SpongeBob fan so I've always wanted to write a puzzle based on the source material. When we needed an answer of NAUTICAL/NAUTILUS I knew it was a great chance. The title is also a reference to a SpongeBob DVD collection, by the way.
I think round 2 is an interesting study. I think it has a lot of individually good puzzles in it, but taken all together they all require a lot of outside googling. Not that's it's a bad thing--round 1 and round 3 are somewhat self-contained when it comes to outside references so it all balances out in the end--but I think if we could do it over though we'd spread them out a little bit more. -JO
