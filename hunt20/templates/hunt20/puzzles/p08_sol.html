The second half of the puzzle comes from the flute line repeating in the background. It's notes are EGGEDCFEAFDAAEFGCAGFFC. There are 22 notes in the loop, 22 total letters in the answers. More interstingly, there are 4 As in the loop and the answer on the staff A (RANK) has 4 letters. This pattern holds for all the notes.
