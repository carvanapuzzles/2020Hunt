Author's Notes
This was originally planned to be in a later round and have all 12 Disney Princesses. The novelty of filling in the crown answers is lost after making the connection the first time, so the puzzle got shrunk and moved into the first round where ideally the majority of them click at the same time in a smaller grid. Like my note from last year, small crosswords also just make great intro puzzles.
This hunt was my first time using QXW, a crossword construction program. It's super cool and I feel like I used about 1% of it, but it really helped in making both this puzzle and Soil Boxes from the third round. Definitely check it out if you ever find yourself needing to create a grid. -JO
