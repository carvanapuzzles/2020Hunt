The flavor text clues that something "cryptic" is going on. The departure statuses are, in fact, cryptic crossword clues. The time of departure is the length of the answer. Answers (with explanation):

Altered the mercury dance? - CHANGED (Altered / Anagram hg+dance)
Nordic heads find it normal now in some hospitals - FINNISH (Nordic / starting letters of "find it normal...")
Neighboring Rob returns a jewelry piece outside Germany - BORDERING (Neighboring / Rob backwards & ring outside DE)
The fattest spouses are in Connecticut - CHUBBIEST (The fattest / hubbies in CT)
Within, MIB lack endurance to burn - BLACKEN (miBLACKENdurance / to burn)
Nap in the noisiest area - SIESTA (Nap / noiSIESTArea)
Herb said, "Senator Booker, behead Xander!" - CORIANDER (Herb / "Cory" + ander)
Strangely, I met one zebra's head in the Pacific or Atlantic - TIMEZONE (Anagram "I met one z" / Pacific or Atlantic)
Powell and co. ate a diner scramble and made a connection on social media - FRIENDED (Anagrammed "diner" inside "Fed" / made a connection on social media)
