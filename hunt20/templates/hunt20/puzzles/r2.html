Round II

WHOOOOSHH!

With a single blast from her atmospheric freeze ray, Dr. Freezio turns the entire stadium into a snowbowl as a blizzard descends out of nowhere. Now you can see why that flier was distributed.

Chaos reigns for a few minutes as everyone rushes for cover and tries to find some snow gear (which unfortunately wasn't on the packing list). You reconvene only to find--gasp!--the Puzzlympic torch has been stolen!

Now you've got a couple problems. Not only is the resulting weather a severe detriment to the planned events (the beach sudoku competition would be absolutely miserable in these conditions), but Puzzlympic tradition demands the torch must be present for every medal ceremony.

It's clear the Doctor wasn't working alone and, to make matters worse, she's disappeared. Time to look for some clues... Maybe you'll be able to track her down.
