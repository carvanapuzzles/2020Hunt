Every image is a state outline that contains a different state's flag on the interior. Identify the outlines and the flags (the outlines states are in alphabetical order).

Outline Flag
Arkansas Colorado
Florida Rhode Island
Idaho Lousiana
Maine Alabama
Mississippi Iowa
New York Indiana
South Dakota Tennessee
Virginia Illinois
Wyoming Alaska

Using the states' two letter abbreviations ("I'll keep it short" from the flavortext), place the interior flag state inside the outer state. This creates a series of 4 letter strings that can be turned into clue answers by adding a letter in the very middle ("still missing some heart"):

Fool N (I (N) N) Y
King M (I (D) A) S
Eccentric W (A (C) K) Y
Rise S (T (A) N) D
Tree M (A (P) L) E
Epic I (L (I) A) D
Profession A (C (T) O) R
Feeble F (R (A) I) L
House V (I (L) L) A

Ordering by original clue order, the middle letters spell ND CAPITAL which clues the answer BISMARCK.

Author's Notes
There were a couple things we tried to emphasize during puzzle construction for this years' hunt. One of them was thematic answers--it's always nice when you get an answer that is vaguely related to the content or theme of the puzzle. We ended up doing a lot of backwards writing where an answer we needed to use would inspire a puzzle. This type of writing is a different sort of challenge but it's fun! The second was a re-upped commitment to tight puzzles. I think we did a decent job of that this year on the whole with a few exceptions, mostly later in the hunt.
We spent a lot of time on the first round puzzles and I'm pretty satisfied with how they turned out on the whole. I think this puzzle is probably the weakest of the bunch, but that's not a big knock. I just really like the others. -JO
